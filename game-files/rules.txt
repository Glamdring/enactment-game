Some governments all work towards better results. However, often it happens that politicians within the same government are rivals.
Be part of such a rivalry.

- 

Start with 5 resource cards and 5 morality.
Before the start draw 3 quality cards. Change up to 2.

Three actions:

- draw three resources (of seven open on the market)
-- you can choose "corruption". If you do, draw 5 resource cards, and lose 2 morality and 1 target
-- at this step you can trade 4 of any resource for an action card
- draw an action card
-- a player cannot have more than 4 action cards in his hand and discards one at random
-- if you are at stage 2, you can trade two stage 1 cards for a new card
- play a card

- play a quality card if you want (can't play more than 2 quality cards in segment 1)
OR
- move your piece (one way forward or one way on target)

Win condition: finalize a reform with 3 or less off-target
Lose condition: if you are left with 0 morality

resources are visible to everyone
